Documented the apply-after-merge deployment convention in `CLAUDE.md` (#34): apply to the live Datadog orgs only *after* a PR merges to `main`, never live-first. The live-first habit caused a provider-orphan deferred-apply chain (monitors referencing `datadog.nsf`/`.eeoc` providers absent from `main` until #32) and cross-PR file-append conflicts across the v0.3.0 monitor PRs (#28–#32). Post-merge apply keeps `main` and live state in lock-step; a zero-diff plan from `main` proves a refactor changed nothing live.
